About Me

My name is Arturo. I was born in Parma, a small city in northern Italy. During high school, I developed a lively passion for physics. I was fascinated by the concept of "energy" and for this reason, in the fourth year, I successfully took the entrance test at the Politecnico di Milano to enter Energetic Engineering. However, in the last year of high school I was introduced to special relativity, which, by connecting mass and energy, destroyed all my beliefs and opened the horizons to a new world. So I decided to study Physics.

My choice led me to leave my city and start traveling around Europe. Not only did my knowledge of physics increase, but I also met wonderful people who along with their culture enriched me as a person as well.

There are still so many questions I would like to have an answer to and I am willing to face all of them in my career, starting from my next adventure: the PhD.
